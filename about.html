About Us

Our Story

Doughlicious was born from a passion for baking and sharing happiness. What started as a small home kitchen experiment in 2015 has grown into a beloved local bakery known for our delicious, handcrafted cookies.

Our founder, Sarah, began baking cookies as a way to bring joy to friends and family during the holidays. The response was so overwhelming that she decided to turn her passion into a business.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of happy customers across Egypt, all while maintaining the same commitment to quality, freshness, and love that went into those first batches of cookies.

Every cookie we make is crafted with care, using only the finest ingredients and traditional baking methods that ensure each bite is a moment of pure delight.
