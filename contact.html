Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer next-day delivery across Cairo and Giza. Free shipping is available for orders over 500 EGP.

Can I place a custom order for special events?

Absolutely! We love creating custom cookie arrangements for weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. Please contact us at least 72 hours in advance.

Are your cookies suitable for people with dietary restrictions?

We offer gluten-free and vegan options. Please specify your dietary requirements when placing your order.

How should I store my cookies to keep them fresh?

Our cookies stay fresh for up to 7 days when stored in an airtight container at room temperature.
